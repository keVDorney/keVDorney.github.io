Below, you'll find links to some of the various posters that I've made over the past few years (as well as some older ones for funsies). These can be great alternatives to the much longer talks I've given, as poster tend to do a good job of keeping me succinct. A rare feat, for sure.

  1. Helicity with a twist: complete control of polarization, divergence, and vortex charge of attosecond, extreme ultraviolet vortices via spin-orbit coupled high harmonic generation
    • This poster was presented at the 2018 Multiphoton Processes Gordon Research Conference in Smithfield, Rhode Island, USA.
  2. Controlling quantum electrodynamics in circularly polarized high harmonic generation: bright, high-energy attosecond waveforms with tailored spectro-temporal properties
    • This poster was presented at the 2017 Trends in Ultrafast Laser Science Conference hosted in JILA at the University of Colorado Boulder in Boulder, Colorado, USA.
  3. Controlling quantum electrodynamics in circularly polarized high harmonic generation: bright, high-energy attosecond waveforms with tailored spectro-temporal properties
    • This poster was presented at the 2017 Colorado Photonics Industry Association Meeting hosted at the University of Colorado Boulder in Boulder, Colorado, USA.
    • This poster was selected for a Best Poster Award by the CPIA selection committee.
  4. Imaging and controlling electron rescattering dynamics in bicircular femtosecond laser fields
    • This poster was presented at the 2016 Trends in Particle Imaging workshop hosted at the Telluride Science and Research Center in Telluride, Colorado, USA.
  5. Generation of angularly-dispersed, circularly polarized high harmonics via non-collinear mixing of intense, ultrafast laser fields
    • This poster was presented at the 2015 JILA Posterfest (internal poster session at JILA) hosted in JILA at the University of Colorado Boulder in Boulder, Colorado, USA.
